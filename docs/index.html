STA 9890 - Statistical Learning for Data Mining

Welcome to the course website for STA 9890 - Statistical Learning for Data Mining (Spring 2025)!

STA 9890 is a survey of Statistical Machine Learning targeted at students in the MS in Business Analytics, MS in Statistics, and MS in Quantitative Methods programs.

This site hosts the unofficial Course Syllabus, Course Policies, and Course Learning Objectives. Official copies of these documents can be found on CUNY Brightspace.

This site also hosts descriptions of the Course Competition and Research Reports (Homework), as well as selected Handouts and Additional Notes that will be useful.
